Other stuff that our customers love about Cogo

Lightning fast search

We have an incredibly fast and sophisticated search engine that indexes every aspect of the content including every word that is found in the file or link.

Clever tagging

To keep things tidy you can add two-level tags to your content. With tags you can add a lot of extra information to the content, like assigning content to an industry, domain, location or customer.

No more folders

Nobody likes digging through thousands of files in a deep unmaintained folder structure, right? Cogo compeletely does away with folders, replacing them with beautiful covers images of your files that you can easily scan at a glance.

Presentation Mode

As you are client facing, with just one click you enter the focused presentation mode of Cogo. This allows you to present the content right in Cogo, without combining everything in a slidedeck.
