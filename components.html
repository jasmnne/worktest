4 column layout

Appears on product page.

Contributor profiles

Everything that’s being published to Cogo has an owner and contributors. Easily explore the work of people with a similar interest through their profiles.

Discuss and improve

Each item has a discussion space, this allows you to get feedback and share experiences to continuously improve the content.

Stars

If you like a particular content item you can star it. This will add the item to ‘my stars’ and will act as a bookmark, additionally all contributors to the item will be rewarded with a star.

Magic

Cogo’s ‘Magic’ option sorts items and collections based on a unique cocktail of popularity, quality, interaction and other criteria. This will make sure the most relevant items are on top.

Notifications

Receive an automatic Slack notification when a new item or collection is created within Cogo.

Starring

When you star an item in Slack, the item will automatically be added to Cogo.

Searching

You can search the  Cogo database from Slack by using the  /search-cogo [keywords] command.

Retracing

Jump directly to the location in your Slack history where the items was posted.

Sign-in

When you enable sign-in with Slack, everyone in your Slack team can access Cogo easily using their Slack accounts.
